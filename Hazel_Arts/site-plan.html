Content

Home page

Why come to this site?

The concept of this page may not be unique, but the content on it is. The creations of Hazel_Arts will not be able to be aquired any where else. So if you are a fan of what she does or want to aquire any of her pieces this will be the only place you can get them.

Why use this site?

People will come to this site for a variaty of different reasons. However the main ones will be for Commission based art requests. This will be the place where a majority of the Buisness of Hazel_Arts will be conducted. It will also serve as a portfolio/gallary of Her art to display, and provide potential contracts and other buisness offers for her tallents.

FAQ

What questions can be answered?

They can expect to know a ball park on price they will pay for the art. Who Hazel_Arts is, what she does, and what her insperations for her peices are. Ways to contact and request things to be created, and estimated times for there compleation.

What info is required to compleate actions?

There will be an account system created. Most if not all orders will be done through the UI on the requests page, where contact info and description/concepts of the item to be made will need to be supplied/refferenced. An understanding that this is a buisness and that things will not be free.
